## ✦ Contribution Flow
1. **Fork the repository** — sanctify your own glyph space.  
2. **Create a branch** — name it with aura resonance (e.g., `dedication-overlay`, `benediction-footer`).  
3. **Add your glyphs, overlays, or pledges** — ensure motif‑driven aura alignment.  
4. **Commit with ceremonial language** — narratable, covenantal messages.  
5. **Open a pull request** — begin with invocation and include your contributor seal.  
6. **Await stakeholder resonance** — merges occur only after covenantal affirmation.
