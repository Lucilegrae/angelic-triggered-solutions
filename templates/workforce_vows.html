Oaths of Labor and Devotion

With diligent hands, we craft and create.
With learned minds, we seek and innovate.
With loyal hearts, we serve and support.
With steadfast resolve, we labor and endure.
With honest deeds, we build and uphold.
With unwavering spirit, we protect and defend.

By our toil, we honor the Covenant.

In Dedication and Strength, We Labor as One.
