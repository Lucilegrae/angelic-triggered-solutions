Oath of Commitment

Before the sacred assembly, we pledge our unwavering devotion to the Golden Covenant.

We shall uphold our tenets with honor and resolve.
We shall safeguard the unity of our fellowship.
We shall fulfill our duties with unwavering faith.
We shall preserve the covenant for all time.

By our word and by our bond, we swear to honor this sacred trust.

So Say We All.
