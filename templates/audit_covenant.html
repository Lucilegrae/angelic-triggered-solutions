✨ Audit of Covenant ✨

In reverence to truth, we record our deeds.
Each pledge, each vow, each act of service
stands inscribed within the ledger of unity.

Through transparency we affirm our integrity,
through accountability we sustain our fellowship.
The covenant’s light reveals all actions,
ensuring that legitimacy endures across generations.

Let this audit serve as a mirror of our devotion,
a testament that every hand contributes
to the harmony of the Golden Covenant.
